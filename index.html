Born in 1992 in a small town, I've always been interested in computers, humans and music. I've studied psychology in high school, guitar since an early age and have always been fiddling with linux.

Around 2009 I've started to put more focus on programming, trying to develop small games with Flash and simple business management tools with Java. This eventually led me to join university (Informatics), which I dropped out of after a couple of years.

Since then my focus has been put on working and self-teaching, mainly web technologies, psychology and expressive medias.

That has now expanded in a share of knowledge, of varying levels, in fields such as:

  • hardware making
  • graphics programming
  • networking and security
  • web development
  • animations and visuals
  • digital music production

I’ve been working for the web industry since 2015, delivering digital experiences and campaigns to thousands (sometimes millions) of users, for a range of clients which goes from independent boutiques to big corporations

Planning, rapid prototyping, crunching deadlines, managing coordination and expectations. Nailing it at times, efficiently recovering from failure at others, always learning.
